Timeline

  • Initial research

    April 2 - September 22 2021

    =nil; Foundation, Mina Foundation and Ethereum Foundation initiated the collaborative research on making in-EVM verification of Mina Protocol's state proof - a core of every Mina-Ethereum bridge in future - possible. Read some details on that in related blog post

  • Detailed Solution Description Development

    September 22 - November 22 2021

    =nil; Foundation's Crypto3 team got to developing Mina Protocol's state proof in-EVM verification detailed solution description development. Read the documentaton to find out about results.

  • Auxiliary Proof Generation Development

    Q4 2021 - Q1 2022

    Mina Protocol's state proof is too expensive to be verified on Ethereum directly. So it requires some preprocessing. In particular, a part of Pickles proof requires to be wrapped with one more proof, proof of successful verification of which could be considered as succesful verification of initial proof on Ethereum. To make this possible, =nil; Foundation got to developing such an auxiliary proof generator development. Try what was accomplished by walking through the in-browser demo.

  • In-EVM Proof Verification Development

    Q4 2021 - Q1 2022

    Mina Protocol's state proof should be verified on Ethereum's side to make sure it proves the actual valid Mina's data. That is why in-EVM Mina's state proof verification mechanism is required to be implemented.
